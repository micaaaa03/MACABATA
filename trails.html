Mount Calavite Trails

This challenging trail takes you to the summit of Mount Calavite, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

Immerse yourself in the lush greenery of Mount Calavite with this trail, where you'll encounter diverse flora and fauna along the way.

Experience the beauty of Mount Calavite's coastal side with this scenic trail, offering views of the ocean and coastal landscapes.

Embark on an evening hike to Sunset Ridge and witness a mesmerizing sunset over Mount Calavite.
